தமிழ்

(Tamil)

Tamil is the world's oldest language as it is over 5,000 years old, having made its first appearance in 300 BC.

However, based on other evidence, scientists believe that it first appeared around 2500 BC. And while it may not be known to a lot of people on our planet, today Tamil is spoken by around 78 million people which makes it the longest surviving language in the world.

Tamil has continued to develop and is now the 20th most commonly-spoken language in the world
